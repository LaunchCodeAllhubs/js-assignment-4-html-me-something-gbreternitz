The Concept

It's a game where the world is a 2d side scroller, and you can occasionally stop the game, pull on the ground's horizon, and launch your little character like a slingshot.

The ArtStyle

Cartoony, likely, but with an element of psychadelic hyper-realism. Idk what that means either, but think FunRun meets Borderlands.
