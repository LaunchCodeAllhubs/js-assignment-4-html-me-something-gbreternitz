The Concept

It's a game where the world is a 3D open world, but you can occasionally reach Jump Points. When you reach a Jump Point, the game clock slows, the camera compresses sideways, and the view transforms into 2D - at which point you can pull down on the ground itself and launch your little character like a slingshot.

This is the main point of the game - find a jump point, launch your character, and collect Skyballs

The ArtStyle

Cartoony, likely, but with an element of psychadelic hyper-realism. Idk what that means either, but think FunRun meets Borderlands.
